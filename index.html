🍽️ What we are building

A beautiful marketplace for real home food.

One platform. Two app experiences. A trusted operating model for home chefs, customers, delivery, and optional grocery support.

👥

For Customers

Browse nearby verified home kitchens, discover daily menus, order fresh meals, pay online, and track delivery.

👩‍🍳

For Homemakers

Create menus, set daily quantity, accept orders, track earnings, and build a trusted food business from home.

Delivery Ready

Optional Groceries

In future, home chefs can optionally request groceries and packaging support through the platform.
